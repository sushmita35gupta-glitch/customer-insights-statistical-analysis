# Customer Insights Statistical Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a statistical investigation of customer demographics, spending behavior, and engagement using Python. The analysis applies descriptive statistics, data visualization, bivariate analysis, and hypothesis testing to uncover meaningful business insights from a customer dataset.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Understand customer demographics and behavior
- Perform descriptive statistical analysis
- Visualize customer spending patterns
- Analyze relationships between variables
- Validate business assumptions using statistical hypothesis tests
- Generate actionable business insights

---

## 💼 Key Business Insights

- Customer spending varies significantly across individuals.
- Gender does not have a statistically significant impact on monthly spending.
- Education level is not a significant predictor of spending behavior.
- Marital status is significantly associated with pet ownership.
- Customer engagement is not significantly influenced by age.
- Behavioral factors provide more valuable insights than demographic characteristics for customer segmentation.
